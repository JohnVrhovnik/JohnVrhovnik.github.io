A student-produced exhibition aimed to invoke thought, creation and contribution in a unique system, bringing together a community by utilizing written language as the fundamental means of interaction.

Design for Engagement

The goal of this project is simply to promote an interaction between a group of individuals who normally wouldn't interact and allow them to share something about themselves with others anonymously. This was achieved by presenting a series of questions in a physical space over the course of a month, allowing viewers to respond to these questions on a small piece of paper on the wall for everyone to see.

The questions presented in the project were "if you had one wish, what would that wish be?", "when you were young, what did you want to be when you grew up?" and "what/who has inspired you in your life?". Responses to these questions varied from serious and sad to funny and ironic. After collecting all of the subject responses, a few dozen of them were published to this website under the subject tabs.
