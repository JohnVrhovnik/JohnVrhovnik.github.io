When you were young, what did you want to be when you grew up?

As we are all starting to establish our career paths, it’s interesting to reflect on how our expectations as children contrast our decisions as adults. Some of us wanted to be the next astronauts, rockstars or professional athletes of our generation, and while few stay faithful to these ambitions, most turn to more realistic choices.

The youthful imagination seems to have some type of beauty in its endless boundaries. We didn’t ever consider how we were going to lead those roles, we just knew it’s what we wanted.
