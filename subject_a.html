If you had one wish, what would that wish be?

As conscious beings, there is always something that we want that we can’t always expect to receive. Some wishes may be modest and easily achieved and others may be an excessive use of one’s imagination.

One individual might wish for an act that works toward the greater good of society, while others might wish for tangible or monetary support in hopes of bettering themselves. Whatever the case, observe how responses vary from person to person in a particularly diverse community.
