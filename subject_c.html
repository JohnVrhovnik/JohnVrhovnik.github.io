What/who has inspired you in your life?

We all find ourselves inspired by things. Whether it be a person or a specific activity of some sort, we are all driven by the things we surround ourselves with. For a lot of people this might be our mentors or a family member but some may reach outside of this.

These inspirations tend to evolve as we grow and move on to new parts of our lives. Try to consider something that has kept you motivated recently or maybe in the earlier years of your life.
